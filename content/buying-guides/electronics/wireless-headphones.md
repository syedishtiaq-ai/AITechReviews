# Best Wireless Headphones 2026

## Overview
Wireless headphones have evolved dramatically with improved battery life, sound quality, and noise cancellation.

## Premium Options
- Industry-leading active noise cancellation
- Superior audio quality
- Extended battery life
- Premium build materials

## Mid-Range Picks
- Excellent value for money
- Good sound quality
- Solid noise cancellation
- Reliable batteries

## Budget-Friendly Options
- Great entry-level performance
- Decent sound quality
- Basic features
- Good durability

## What to Consider When Buying
1. **Sound Quality** - Treble, bass, and clarity
2. **Noise Cancellation** - Both ANC and passive isolation
3. **Battery Life** - Usage hours and charging time
4. **Comfort** - Fit, padding, and weight
5. **Features** - Controls, transparency mode, app support
