# Best Washing Machines 2026

## Overview
Modern washing machines offer various designs suited to different laundry needs and space constraints.

## Front-Load Washers
- Superior water efficiency
- Larger capacity options
- Advanced fabric care
- Higher initial cost

## Top-Load Washers
- Traditional design
- Lower cost options
- Faster wash cycles
- Good for heavier loads

## Compact & Portable Options
- Space-saving design
- Apartment-friendly
- Portable water connections
- Limited capacity

## Important Features
1. **Capacity** - Load size in cubic feet
2. **Cycles & Settings** - Wash programs available
3. **Water Usage** - Gallons per cycle
4. **Spin Speed** - RPM for moisture removal
5. **Smart Features** - App control and alerts

## Maintenance Tips
- Clean water inlet filters regularly
- Use recommended detergent amounts
- Run maintenance cycles monthly
- Check hoses for wear
