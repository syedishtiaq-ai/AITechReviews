# Elden Ring Achievement Hunting Guide

## Overview
Elden Ring features a comprehensive achievement/trophy system with multiple hidden achievements that challenge players across different playstyles.

## Main Quest Achievements
- Great Runes achievements
- Boss defeating achievements
- NPC questline achievements

## Combat Achievements
- Defeat unique bosses across the Lands Between
- Master different weapon types
- Complete challenge runs

## Exploration Achievements
- Find hidden items and locations
- Discover secret areas
- Complete map coverage

## Tips for Achievement Hunting
1. Use a map guide for hidden achievements
2. Save before critical story points
3. Plan multiple playthroughs for conflicting achievements
4. Join community guides for location confirmations
