# Best Armor Sets in AC Odyssey

Choosing the right armor set is important for maximizing your build in **Assassin's Creed Odyssey**.

## Best Assassin Armor

### Pirate Set

Best for stealth builds.

Bonuses:

- Assassin damage
- Adrenaline generation

## Best Warrior Armor

### Spartan War Hero Set

Perfect for melee combat.

Bonuses:

- Warrior damage
- Weapon damage

## Best Hunter Armor

### Artemis Set

Ideal for bow-focused builds.

Bonuses:

- Hunter damage
- Headshot damage

## How to Get Legendary Armor

Legendary armor comes from:

- Cultists
- Mercenaries
- Mythical creatures
