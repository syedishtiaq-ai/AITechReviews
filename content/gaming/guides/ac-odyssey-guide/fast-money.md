# Fastest Way to Earn Money in AC Odyssey

If you are struggling to upgrade your gear or ship in **Assassin's Creed Odyssey**, this guide explains the **fastest ways to earn drachmae**.

Drachmae are required for:

- Ship upgrades
- Weapon upgrades
- Armor improvements
- Blacksmith services

Using the methods below, you can earn large amounts of money quickly.

---

## Quick Overview

Best money methods in AC Odyssey:

- Athens wall runs
- Mercenary farming
- Fort raids
- Selling epic gear

---

## Athens Wall Run (Best Early Game Method)

Athens has one of the **longest continuous walls** in the game.

This makes it one of the best locations to farm weapons and armor quickly.

### Steps

1. Start at the main Athens gate
2. Run along the city wall eliminating guards
3. Loot all weapons and armor
4. Fast travel away
5. Repeat the run

You can earn **10,000–20,000 drachmae per run** depending on loot drops.

---

## Mercenary Farming

Mercenaries drop valuable loot including:

- Epic weapons
- Rare armor
- Large drachmae rewards

### How to farm mercenaries

1. Increase your bounty level
2. Wait for mercenaries to hunt you
3. Defeat them in combat
4. Sell their gear at a blacksmith

Mercenaries are one of the **most reliable sources of epic gear** in the game.

---

## Fort Raids

Large forts contain many valuable loot sources including:

- Nation chests
- Weapon racks
- Resource crates

### Best strategy

- Use stealth to clear enemies
- Loot every weapon rack
- Open nation chests
- Sell extra gear

---

## Selling Epic Gear

Epic gear sells for significantly more than common items.

Best approach:

- Keep legendary gear
- Sell epic gear
- Dismantle common gear for materials

This strategy maximizes both **drachmae and crafting materials**.

---

## Rewards

Using these methods you can earn enough resources to upgrade:

- Ship hull
- Arrow damage
- Armor sets
- Legendary weapons
