# Easiest Way to Upgrade Your Ship in AC Odyssey

Ship upgrades require large amounts of resources in **Assassin's Creed Odyssey**.

This guide explains how to farm them quickly.

## Required Resources

Ship upgrades need:

- Olive wood
- Iron metal
- Obsidian glass
- Ancient tablets
- Drachmae

## Best Resource Farming Methods

### Sink Pirate Ships

Pirate ships give large rewards.

Look for ships around:

- Kephallonia
- Korinthia
- Aegean Sea

### Board Instead of Destroy

Boarding ships gives:

- More loot
- Extra resources
- Additional drachmae

### Loot Nation Chests

Nation chests give:

- Large resource bundles
- Drachmae
- Rare items

## Priority Ship Upgrades

Upgrade these first:

1. Arrow damage
2. Ship hull
3. Javelin damage
4. Fire attacks
