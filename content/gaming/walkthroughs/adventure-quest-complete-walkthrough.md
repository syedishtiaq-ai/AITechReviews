# Complete Walkthrough - Adventure Quest Story Guide

## Introduction
Welcome to the complete walkthrough for Adventure Quest! This guide will help you progress through every level, solve all puzzles, and defeat every boss encounter.

## Chapter 1: The Beginning

### Level 1-1: Starting Village
**Objectives:**
- Gather 3 potions
- Talk to the village elder
- Collect the ancient key

**Walkthrough:**
1. Start at the village center
2. Head north to the blacksmith
3. Collect potions: one inside the shop, one in the garden, one by the well
4. Return to village center and talk to the elder
5. Receive ancient key

### Level 1-2: Forest Path
**Objectives:**
- Navigate through the forest
- Defeat forest guardian
- Collect 5 gems

**Enemy Strategy:**
- Forest Guardian has 3 attack phases
- Use fire spells in phase 1
- Switch to ice spells in phase 2
- Physical attacks effective in phase 3

**Gem Locations:**
- Behind large tree (north area)
- Under stone bridge
- Inside hollow log
- By crystal stream
- Hidden in cave entrance

## Chapter 2: The Mountains

### Level 2-1: Mountain Path
**Objectives:**
- Climb the mountain
- Find checkpoint shelter
- Solve ice puzzle

**Ice Puzzle Solution:**
1. Move ice blocks to form bridge
2. Place blocks in this order: Left, Right, Middle
3. Cross to reach checkpoint

### Level 2-2: Summit Battle
**Boss Fight: Mountain Titan**

**Boss Stats:**
- HP: 500
- Weakness: Lightning magic
- Special Attack: Avalanche (avoid by moving to sides)

**Strategy:**
- Phase 1: Attack with lightning 5-6 times
- Phase 2: Dodge avalanche, counter-attack
- Phase 3: Use ultimate ability when available
- Victory: Obtain Mountain Stone

## Chapter 3: The Mysterious Castle

### Level 3-1: Castle Exterior
**Objectives:**
- Find 3 door keys
- Activate 2 pressure plates
- Solve mirror puzzle

**Key Locations:**
1. Key 1: Inside fountain puzzle room
2. Key 2: Behind secret wall (bomb required)
3. Key 3: Guarded by shade enemy

**Pressure Plate Solution:**
- Plate 1: Activate from eastern door
- Plate 2: Activate from western door
- Both must activate within 30 seconds

### Level 3-2: Castle Interior
**Objectives:**
- Navigate maze corridors
- Defeat shadow knights
- Find treasure vault

**Treasure Locations:**
- Main hall: Gold chest
- West wing: Legendary sword
- East wing: Magic amulet

### Level 3-3: Final Boss - Shadow King
**Boss Stats:**
- HP: 800
- Weakness: Holy magic
- Immunity: Dark magic

**Strategy:**
- Phase 1: Use holy attacks, avoid shadow tentacles
- Phase 2: Heal when below 50% HP
- Phase 3: Use group ultimate ability
- Victory: Game complete!

## Tips and Tricks

### Combat Tips
- Always keep your health above 30%
- Use special abilities wisely
- Exploit enemy weaknesses
- Heal before major battles

### Puzzle Solutions
- Read all hint plaques carefully
- Save before difficult sections
- Experiment with different approaches
- Use the hint system if stuck 10+ minutes

### Collection Tips
- Backtrack after obtaining new abilities
- Hidden items often require special powers
- Talk to NPCs for hints
- Check every corner thoroughly

## Achievement Guide

### Easy Achievements
- Complete Chapter 1
- Defeat 10 enemies
- Collect 50 gems

### Hard Achievements
- Speedrun mode (complete in under 2 hours)
- No damage run (boss fights without taking damage)
- Collect all secret items

## Conclusion
Congratulations on completing Adventure Quest! You've defeated the shadow king and saved the realm. Enjoy your victory and consider challenging yourself with harder difficulty modes!
