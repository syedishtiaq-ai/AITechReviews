# Dungeon Crawler Pro Tips - Speed Run and 100% Guide

## Game Overview
Dungeon Crawler is an action RPG that challenges players to explore dangerous dungeons, defeat monsters, and collect treasure. This guide covers both speedrun strategies and 100% completion methods.

## General Progression Tips

### Early Game (Levels 1-10)
**Recommended Setup:**
- Focus on gathering basic equipment
- Don't farm too much at start
- Skip optional side quests initially
- Focus main boss battles

**Key Items to Obtain:**
- Wooden Sword (starter weapon)
- Leather Armor (basic protection)
- Health Potions (essential)

### Mid Game (Levels 10-50)
**Strategic Approach:**
- Balance main quest with side content
- Farm specific areas for rare drops
- Upgrade equipment regularly
- Learn enemy patterns

**Important Unlocks:**
- Magic spell system
- Special abilities
- Advanced equipment tiers

### Late Game (Levels 50-100)
**Optimization:**
- Focus on endgame content
- Grind for legendary equipment
- Complete optional challenges
- Prepare for final bosses

## 100% Completion Checklist

### Collectibles Required
- **Treasure Chests**: 150 (unlocks secret items)
- **Monster Slays**: 500+ (completes bestiary)
- **NPC Quests**: 75 (hidden story content)
- **Secret Locations**: 50 (hidden areas)

### Treasure Locations by Zone

**Zone 1: Forest Dungeon**
- Chest 1: Right passage from entrance
- Chest 2: Behind waterfall (use water spell)
- Chest 3: Hidden in tree hollow

**Zone 2: Mountain Cavern**
- Chest 1: At snowy peak
- Chest 2: Behind ice wall (melt with fire)
- Chest 3: Guarded by ice golem

**Zone 3: Underground City**
- Chest 1: Ancient library
- Chest 2: Treasure vault
- Chest 3: Forgotten crypt

### Secret Areas Guide

**Hidden Areas Unlock Progression:**
1. Forest Secret: Ice Cave (requires level 20)
2. Mountain Secret: Lava Chambers (requires level 40)
3. Underground Secret: Crystal Palace (requires level 60)

**Location Tips:**
- Listen for environmental clues
- Walk walls looking for hidden passages
- Use item combinations on obstacles
- Talk to NPCs about rumors

## Speedrun Strategy

### Any% Route (Fastest)
**Estimated Time**: 2-3 hours

**Route:**
1. Forest Dungeon: Skip all treasures, rush to boss
2. Mountain Cavern: Collect speed-boost item only
3. Underground City: Main path only
4. Final Boss: Direct approach
5. Victory

**Speed Techniques:**
- Abuse item duplication glitch (if patched, skip)
- Use speed boost potions
- Optimal input buffering
- Skip all dialogue

### 100% Route (All Content)
**Estimated Time**: 15-18 hours

**Additional Requirements:**
- Collect all 150 treasure chests
- Complete all optional dungeons
- Talk to all NPCs
- Solve all optional puzzles
- Defeat optional bosses

## Boss Fight Strategies

### Boss 1: Forest Guardian
**Speedrun Tactic:**
- Don't block, focus on DPS
- Use fire spell x2 for phase skip
- Victory time: 3 minutes

**100% Method:**
- Learn all attack patterns
- Defeat without items for achievement
- Collect special drop

### Boss 2: Mountain Titan
**Speedrun Tactic:**
- Ice spell spam until defeat
- Ignore mechanics, power through
- Victory time: 4 minutes

**100% Method:**
- Defeat on hard difficulty
- Mirror boss battle (reverse roles)
- Ultimate achievement available

### Boss 3: Shadow Lord (Final)
**Speedrun Tactic:**
- Use ultimate ability immediately
- Exploit phase transition bug
- Victory time: 5-6 minutes

**100% Method:**
- Defeat all previous optional bosses first
- Complete true final form battle
- Unlock true ending
